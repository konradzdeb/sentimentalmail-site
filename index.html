FAQ

Short answers to common questions.

Does Mail.App send my emails anywhere?

No. The intended design is on-device analysis. Your email content stays on your Mac.

Do I need to use a new inbox?

No. Mail.App integrates with Apple Mail as an extension, so you keep your existing workflow.

Is sentiment analysis always correct?

No. Treat the indicators as a prompt. You make the final call, especially for high-stakes messages.

Will it slow down Mail?

It is designed to be lightweight and unobtrusive. Performance depends on your Mac and mailbox size.
