Capabilities

Seamlessly integrated with Apple Mail. Sentimental scores the sentiment of your messages on your Mac and presents clear markers inline so you can triage with confidence.

Seamless Apple Mail integration

Works inside Apple Mail without changing how you read, search, or organise your inbox.

Sentiment scoring with thresholds

Scores messages and lets you choose thresholds so markers match your inbox and tolerance for negative, neutral, or positive tone.

Marker style selection

Select how sentiment shows up: colour semantics or flag-style markers, depending on how you triage.

Message statistics & trends

Review positive, neutral, and negative counts over time with date-range charts.

Sender filters

Include or exclude sender patterns so scoring focuses on the mail you care about.

On-device processing

Analysis happens on your machine. No message content is sent out and Sentimental works offline.

Clean text extraction

Extracts subject and body, removes HTML markup, and normalises whitespace for consistent scoring.

Localized UI

Localized UI so the interface feels native on your Mac.

Download

Install in minutes. Enable the extension in Mail → Settings → Extensions.

Enable the Mail extension

  • 1Open Mail and choose Mail → Settings.
  • 2Open the Extensions tab and tick Sentimental.
  • 3Close Settings and return to your inbox. The change applies immediately.

If Sentimental is not listed, open the app once, then return to Mail → Settings → Extensions.
