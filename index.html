Capabilities

Seamlessly integrated with Apple Mail. Sentimental scores the sentiment of your messages on your Mac and presents clear markers inline so you can triage with confidence.

Seamless Apple Mail integration

Works inside Apple Mail without changing how you read, search, or organise your inbox.

Sentiment scoring with thresholds

Scores messages and lets you choose thresholds so markers match your inbox and tolerance for “negative” or “urgent” tone.

Marker style selection

Select how sentiment shows up: colour semantics or flag-style markers, depending on how you triage.

On-device processing

Analysis happens on your machine. No message content is sent out and Sentimental works offline.

Clean text extraction

Extracts subject and body, removes HTML markup, and normalises whitespace for consistent scoring.

Localisation-ready

User-facing strings are prepared for translation and multi-language support.
